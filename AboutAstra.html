About Astra

Meet Astra

Astra is your smart, voice-enabled AI assistant designed to help you manage daily tasks, answer your questions, and make your digital experience more interactive and engaging. Whether you're setting reminders, checking the time, or just chatting for fun, Astra is here to assist you 24/7.

Key Features

Astra is equipped with a wide range of features, including:

  • Speech recognition and text-to-speech responses
  • Real-time clock, alarm, and countdown timer
  • Task manager, daily planner, and to-do list
  • Unit and currency converters
  • Customizable themes and voice personalities
  • Memory-based responses and personalization

Vision & Goals

The vision behind Astra is to create an intelligent virtual companion that blends utility with personality. Our goal is to evolve Astra into a highly responsive, emotional, and helpful AI that enhances your productivity and makes your daily digital interaction more intuitive.
