Help Guide - How to Use Astra

Getting Started

Welcome to Astra, your AI-powered voice assistant! To get started, make sure your microphone is enabled. Astra listens to your voice commands and responds with helpful actions or information.

Voice Commands

You can talk to Astra naturally. Some examples:

  • “What time is it?”
  • “Set a timer for 5 minutes.”
  • “Add ‘Buy groceries’ to my to-do list.”
  • “Tell me a fun fact.”
  • “Switch theme to dark/light.”
  • “Set an alarm for 7 AM.”

Features You Can Use

  • Clock & Date: Ask the current time or date.
  • Timer & Alarms: Set and manage timers or alarms using voice.
  • To-Do List: Add, view, or remove tasks.
  • Daily Planner: Organize your schedule by speaking to Astra.
  • Converters: Convert currencies or units quickly.
  • Personality & Themes: Customize Astra’s voice or theme.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your microphone is not blocked or disabled.
  • Refresh the page if Astra stops listening.
  • Ensure browser permissions are granted for voice input.
