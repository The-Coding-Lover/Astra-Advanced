Astra is a voice-enabled AI chatbot designed to help with daily tasks, reminders, and conversations. This manual helps you get started and explore Astra’s features.

4. Features Overview

  • Voice Activation: Say "Hey Astra" or tap mic.
  • Text Input: Type directly in the chat box.
  • Utilities: Timer, Alarm, To-Do List, Date/Time, Calculator
  • Customization: Voice, Theme, Personality
  • Learning: Astra remembers user preferences.

5. Troubleshooting

Issue: Voice not working?
Solution: Check microphone permission in your browser.

Issue: Not responding?
Solution: Refresh the page or retype your query.
